What it is for

At Buddy Adoption Centre, we are on a mission to provide a loving and secure environment for animals in need. Our goal is to ensure that every animal that comes through our doors finds a forever home filled with care and affection. We firmly believe that every animal deserves a second chance at happiness. We connect with local adoption centres and provides them a platform to share the description of pets which are available for adoption.

What We Do

  • Rescue: We rescue animals from various situations, including strays, abuse cases, and those abandoned by their owners.

  • Rehabilitate: Our experienced team provides necessary medical care, attention, and rehabilitation to ensure that each animal is ready for their forever home.

  • Rehome: We facilitate the adoption of our rescued animals, matching them with loving families where they can thrive and experience the care they deserve.

Join Us

We invite you to be a part of our mission. Whether you are looking to adopt a new family member, volunteer your time, or make a donation, your support is invaluable. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of these wonderful animals.
